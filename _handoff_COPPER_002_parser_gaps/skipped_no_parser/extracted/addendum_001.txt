Phase B: Wired network re-termination.  
 
The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, and equipment required for 
the re-termination and testing of existing copper infrastructure to new 
keystone based patch panels in new or existing network cabinets / racks.  
 
Contractor work includes the removal of old patch panels, installation of new 
keystone based patch panels, and the re-termination of existing copper 
wiring at patch panels in MDF and IDF locations. Work will also include WPS 
specified end points that have been pre-determined as faulty and need 
replacement.  
Installation of a new network cabinet in basement IDF Room 010 will be 
required. Contractor will test all connections for full functionality.   
Required hardware listed in parts/specifications section.  MDF, and IDF 
locations are listed in Appendix A."
